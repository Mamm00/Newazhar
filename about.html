About the Founder

Shady Anwar &
Al Azhar Tex

Founded by Shady Anwar in the heart of Zagazig, Al Azhar Tex has grown from a modest fabric gallery into one of Egypt's most trusted wholesale fabric suppliers. With a hands-on approach to sourcing — personally checking every roll for quality, drape and consistency — the store has built an unmatched reputation across Al Sharqia governorate and beyond.

Our gallery stocks 20+ premium fabric lines: from the whisper-light Chiffon 180 to the lustrous Bubble Satin, from the structured ITY Lickra to the fashion-forward Warsaw and Lexus. Every piece is available by the roll for wholesale buyers.

"I don't just sell fabric. I sell the confidence a woman feels when she wears it."

— Shady Anwar, Founder

Our Journey · مسيرتنا

Milestones

1995
The First Bolt
Shady Anwar opened the first Al Azhar Tex gallery in Zagazig, selling women's fabrics from a modest showroom in the market quarter.
2005
Wholesale Expansion
Shifted focus to wholesale, building relationships with dressmakers and boutiques across Al Sharqia governorate.
2015
New Showroom Opens
Moved to a larger, purpose-built gallery space where clients browse 20+ fabric lines in comfort.
2024
Online Presence
Launched Al Azhar Tex online to serve wholesale clients nationally, with WhatsApp ordering available 6 days a week.
